Although we at HMIGROUPEC, LLC endeavor to find properties for our clients that are in the best possible condition, sometimes the best investment opportunities are found in hidden gems referred to “diamonds in the rough.” These properties could be dated, old, awkward or have other challenges that require renovation, creativity and a little out-of-the-box thinking.

In order to keep our clients updated on the status of their property’s renovations, and at what stage of the make ready process we are in, we post pictures in this area of our website weekly.
Current Project: 504 Mingea Street Petersburg, Virginia 

In early 2019, we were contacted by the Smart Investment Group, LLC of Simi Valley, California and asked to assist them in locating and purchasing property in the Central Virginia area also known as the Piedmont.
We are happy to say that were able to assist the Smart Investment Group, LLC in acquiring a property in Petersburg, Virginia. As their representatives, we were able to negotiate a great deal for a two-bedroom, one bath single-family home.
The asking price for the property was $39,000 prior to us sending in our renovation team to do a thorough inspection of the property. After presenting the owner with an itemized report on the condition of the property along with photos, we were able to negotiate a purchase price of $26,000 of the property.
Renovation:
We knew going in that we had a renovation budget of $10,000.00. But with some creative use of resources and proper management of client funds our renovation team was able to reduce the cost of the renovation budget to $8,500.00 (Note: We solicited bids from a few local contractors to test our numbers and the lowest bid we received was for $15,000).
WE can’t guarantee that we will be able to reduce every client’s cost by this much, but we always make a effort to secure the best prices, best materials, and best turn-around time for our clients in order for them to maximize the return on their investment.
